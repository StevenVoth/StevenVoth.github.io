Key Aviation Terms

  • VFR - Visual Flight Rules

    Operating an aircraft under weather conditions clear enough to see and navigate visually.

  • IFR - Instrument Flight Rules

    Flying aircraft under conditions where navigation relies on instruments rather than visual references.

  • Autopilot

    A system that can control the aircraft's trajectory automatically without continuous pilot input.
