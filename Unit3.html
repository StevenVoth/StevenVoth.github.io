Key Aviation Terms

  • VFR - Visual Flight Rules

    A set of regulations under which a pilot operates an aircraft in weather conditions clear enough to see where the aircraft is going.

  • IFR - Instrument Flight Rules

    Regulations and procedures for flying aircraft without the assumption that pilots will be able to see and avoid obstacles visually.

  • Autopilot

    A system used to control the trajectory of an aircraft without constant 'hands-on' control by a human operator.
